Tune Hunt

Discover hidden treasures within millions of songs that apart from the artist itself probably no-one else has ever listened to before.

This is the opposite of all other recommendation engines, most of the time we choose songs that no-one would have ever recommended to you. Only from time to time without you knowing you'll be also presented with music other users have come across and liked on their tune hunts.
